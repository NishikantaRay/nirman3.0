NIRMAN 2.0 is Silicon Robotics Club and Circuit Club annual-inter college tech event. As the second iteration of this event, NIRMAN 2.0 will bring together over 450+ student tech enthusiasts from across the country for a hybrid, weekend-long high tech event. We emphasize learning through doing, and welcome students regardless of any technical or management background. Your support will help us provide our hackers with materials, prizes ,food and a lot more.
